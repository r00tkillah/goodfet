Download

A stable release is always available at the following URL, which is uploaded when all test cases pass. At some point, one of these distributions will become the first official release.
http://goodfet.sourceforge.net/dist/goodfet.zip

Similarly, the firmware can be updated to the dist version with 

goodfet.bsl --fromweb

As a third choice, you may follow the subversion feed. Every commit ought to work, but no guarantee is made as to its reliability or safety. 

svn co https://goodfet.svn.sourceforge.net/svnroot/goodfet
