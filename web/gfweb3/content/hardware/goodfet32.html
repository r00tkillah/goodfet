GoodFET32

The GoodFET32 is the twenty-fifth hardware revision of the GoodFET. It is easier to solder than the GoodFET4x series, and the physical USB A connector relieves some of the construction hassles of the GoodFET31.

CAD Files

Eagle CAD 6.x files for the design can be found in /branches/goodfet32 of the subversion repository, or as goodfet32.zip.
