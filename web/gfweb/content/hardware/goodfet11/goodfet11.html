GoodFET11

The GoodFET11 is the second hardware revision of the GoodFET. It fixes minor errata from the GoodFET10 but adds no new features other than an optional 32KHz crystal.

CAD Files

Eagle CAD 5.x files for the design can be found in /branches/goodfet11 of Subversion, or as goodfet11.zip.
