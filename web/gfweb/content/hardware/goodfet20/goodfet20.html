GoodFET20

The GoodFET20 is the third hardware revision of the GoodFET. In addition to the low frequency crystal, it adds a high frequency crystal for speeds up to 8MHz and an optional 74HC4053 analog bidirectional MUX for voltage glitching.

CAD Files

Eagle CAD 5.x files for the design can be found in /branches/goodfet20 of the subversion repository, or as goodfet20.zip.
