goodfet.zensys

Client

Usage: goodfet.zensys verb [objects]

goodfet.zensys info
goodfet.zensys infodata
goodfet.zensys writeinfodata 0x$infodata [xtal_freq]
goodfet.zensys lockbits
goodfet.zensys protect_flash
goodfet.zensys protect_page0
goodfet.zensys bootsize $size
goodfet.zensys dump $foo.rom [0x$start 0x$stop]
goodfet.zensys erase all [xtal_freq]
goodfet.zensys erase flash [xtal_freq]
goodfet.zensys erase page $page_number [xtal_freq]
goodfet.zensys flash $foo.rom [xtal_freq 0x$start 0x$stop]
goodfet.zensys verify $foo.rom [0x$start 0x$stop]

Notes

Please note that the chip/module is NOT powered by the usual Vcc pins due to specific timings that have to be respected during the power-up phase.

Note also that the !RST pin of the ASIC is directly connected to GND for the same reasons.
